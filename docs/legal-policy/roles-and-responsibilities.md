# Roles and Responsibilities

This page is aligned to the [Task RACI Matrix](/docs/legal-policy/role-task-matrix/), which is the source of truth for task-level role assignments.

## Standard Roles (RACI-Aligned)

- **Executive Sponsor (`SP`)**: mission alignment, major gate authorization, and final accountability.
- **Program Manager (`PM`)**: orchestration across domains, readiness management, and delivery accountability.
- **Analyst (`AN`)**: problem framing, evidence generation, and performance interpretation.
- **Data Engineer (`DE`)**: data pipelines, integration, deployment execution, and observability support.
- **Data Scientist (`DS`)**: model design, experimentation, validation, and drift/retraining criteria.
- **Data Steward (`ST`)**: data governance, quality controls, metadata, and policy-aligned data handling.
- **Reviewer/Oversight (`RV`)**: independent review, compliance validation, and gate quality checks.
- **Platform Admin (`AD`)**: platform configuration, identity/access controls, and operational guardrails.

## RACI Operating Rules

- Every IAF task should have one accountable role (`A`) and at least one responsible role (`R`).
- Domain gate approvals must identify approver role and timestamp.
- Service outcomes must identify requestor and assigned owner.
- Compliance artifacts must identify accountable sign-off authority.

## Task-Level Assignment Reference

- For Domain I-VII task-level assignments and descriptions, use the [Task RACI Matrix](/docs/legal-policy/role-task-matrix/).
- Task hyperlinks across services and demo pages resolve to that matrix.
