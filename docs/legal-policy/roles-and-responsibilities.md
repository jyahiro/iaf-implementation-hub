# Roles and Responsibilities

This page is aligned to the [Task RACI Matrix](/docs/legal-policy/role-task-matrix/), which is the source of truth for task-level role assignments. Role names follow the **Microsoft Team Data Science Process (TDSP)** for analytics products; see [By role](/docs/start-here/by-role/) for TDSP definitions and hub key mapping.

## Multiple roles per person

The Hub **explicitly allows one person to hold multiple roles** at the same time. Role assignments represent **accountability for duties** in the analytics lifecycle, not exclusive HR job codes. When a user acts under more than one role, every approval, artifact, service request, and audit event must record **which role context** applied. Where agency policy requires separation of duties, enforce it through **approval routing, independent review, or compensating controls**—not by forbidding multi-role identity unless your agency mandates single-role binding.

For **functional roles** (Data Steward, Model Architect, Compliance Lead, Project Lead) mapped to all seven IAF domains with **Law, Regulation, and Policy (LRP)** per cell and **governance triggers**, see the [Functional roles, IAF domains, and LRP governance matrix](./functional-role-domain-lrp-matrix.md).

## Standard roles (TDSP-aligned RACI codes)

- **Group Manager (`GM`)**: business outcomes, priorities, resourcing; major gate authorization and accountability.
- **Team Lead (`TL`)**: day-to-day execution leadership, quality, reviews, and technical gate readiness.
- **Project Lead (`PL`)**: business understanding, scope, success metrics, stakeholder communications, and delivery orchestration.
- **Application Developer (`AP`)**: integration of analytics into applications, APIs, and mission workflows; implementation and acceptance support.
- **Data Scientist (`DS`)**: modeling, experimentation, validation, monitoring criteria, and method documentation.
- **DevOps Engineer (`DO`)**: pipelines, environments, deployment, observability, and operational controls for releases.
- **Data Engineer (`DE`)**: data acquisition, transformation, quality, lineage, and governance-aligned data handling.
- **Platform Admin (`AD`)**: platform configuration, identity/access controls, and operational guardrails (hub operations outside the core TDSP customer team).

## RACI Operating Rules

- Every IAF task should have one accountable role (`A`) and at least one responsible role (`R`).
- Domain gate approvals must identify approver role and timestamp.
- Service outcomes must identify requestor and assigned owner.
- Compliance artifacts must identify accountable sign-off authority.
