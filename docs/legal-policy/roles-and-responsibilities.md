# Roles and Responsibilities

Use these standard roles to keep ownership and approvals consistent across teams.

## Role Definitions

- **Executive Sponsor:** accountable for strategic alignment and final authorization.
- **Program Manager:** accountable for delivery orchestration and gate readiness.
- **Analyst:** accountable for analytics framing, methodology, and evidence outputs.
- **Data Steward:** accountable for data governance, quality, access, and controls.
- **Reviewer/Oversight:** accountable for compliance validation and independent review.
- **Admin:** accountable for platform configuration, access controls, and operations support.

## Approval Responsibilities

- Domain gate approvals must identify approver role and timestamp.
- Service outcomes must identify requestor and assigned owner.
- Compliance artifacts must identify accountable sign-off authority.
