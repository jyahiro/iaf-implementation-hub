# Compliance Checkpoints

This matrix defines required policy and governance checkpoints by domain stage.

| Domain | Required Artifact | Approval Authority | Policy Basis |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Domain I | M-13-13 (Open Data Policy) Governance Checklist | Sponsor / Program Lead | Evidence Act, M-13-13 (Open Data Policy) |
| Domain III | OPEN Data Act / FDS Compliance Record | Data Steward / Reviewer | OPEN Government Data Act, Federal Data Strategy |
| Domain V-VI | AI RMF / M-24-10 Assurance Case | Technical Reviewer / Authorizing Official | NIST AI RMF, M-24-10 |
| All Domains | Domain checklist and approval record | Assigned gate approver | NIST 800-53-aligned controls |

## Records and Retention

- Maintain artifact versions in repository history.
- Preserve approval decisions with timestamp and role.
- Ensure audit events are exportable for oversight review.
