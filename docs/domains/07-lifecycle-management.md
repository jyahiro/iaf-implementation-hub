# Lifecycle Management

This domain governs ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and evolution of analytics products after deployment. It includes performance tracking, drift detection, retraining, change control, and retirement planning to sustain value over time.

In federal data governance, lifecycle management supports accountability by ensuring analytic systems remain compliant, auditable, and fit for purpose as conditions change. For the Department of the Army's data strategy, it enables long-term mission impact by institutionalizing continuous improvement and responsible stewardship across the analytics lifecycle.
