# Service 01: Problem Framing Facilitation

## Purpose
Establish a clear, decision-relevant business problem statement and stakeholder alignment for Domain I readiness.

## When to Use
- Problem statements are ambiguous or not decision-actionable.
- Stakeholders disagree on scope, outcomes, or priorities.
- Sponsor alignment is not yet formalized.

## Required Inputs
- Current problem statement draft
- Stakeholder list
- Relevant strategy or policy context

## Outputs
- Validated Domain I problem brief
- Stakeholder alignment record
- Go/No-Go recommendation for Domain II

## Standard Turnaround
- 5-10 business days, depending on stakeholder availability.
