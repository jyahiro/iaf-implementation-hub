# Service 05: Lifecycle Monitoring and Recalibration Support

## Purpose
Provide sustained Domain VII oversight support for performance monitoring, recalibration, and business-value continuity.

## When to Use
- Performance drift or operational side effects are detected.
- Recalibration cadence is undefined.
- Teams need structured lifecycle governance.

## Required Inputs
- Monitoring metrics and thresholds
- Incident/performance history
- Current maintenance and retraining procedures

## Outputs
- Lifecycle oversight plan
- Recalibration decision guidance
- Updated risk and documentation maintenance actions
