# By Role

## Executive Sponsor

- Approve problem framing and strategic outcomes.
- Confirm risk posture and decision rights.
- Authorize progression at major governance gates.

## Program Manager

- Coordinate domain progression and artifact completion.
- Ensure services are requested with required inputs.
- Track readiness, dependencies, and SLA expectations.

## Analyst and Data Team

- Produce evidence artifacts and technical documentation.
- Validate data quality, methodology, and model performance.
- Record assumptions, limitations, and residual risks.

## Reviewer and Oversight

- Validate compliance checkpoints and control evidence.
- Confirm legal/policy alignment and auditability.
- Approve, reject, or return gates for remediation.
